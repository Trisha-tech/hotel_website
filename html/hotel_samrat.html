HOTEL SAMRAT DELHI

50-B,Chanakyapuri,Kautilya Marg,New Delhi


Location:
Situated within 4 km range of Dilli Haat, Hotel Samrat offers a health club, salon, business centre, banquet halls, restaurant and Wi-Fi internet.

Hotel Samrat, located in New Delhi, is a 4 star property that offers comfortable accommodation for both leisure and business guests. This hotel has Executive Rooms, which are air conditioned, well-furnished and equipped with modern amenities, like Wi-Fi internet, plasma TV, electronic safe, business desk, mini bar, hair dryer and iron on request. It lies at a short distance from Connaught Place, Dilli Haat, Lotus Temple, Qutub Minar and Akshardham Temple.

There is a well-equipped health club - Fitness Studio within the premises; a beauty salon, which offers all types of beauty treatment like facial and hair cut and a business centre for meetings and seminars. It has two banquet halls Kautilya and Chanakya Chandragupta for theme parties and mega events.

In terms of dining, it has a restaurant called Chakras, where guests can relish a choice of delightful delicacies.

Visit the nearby Qutub Minar, an architectural wonder of the Mughal era.
