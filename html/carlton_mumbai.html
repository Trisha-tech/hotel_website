CARLTON HOTEL - COLABA MUMBAI

Florence House, No.12,Mere Weather Road, Behind Taj Mahal Hotel,Colaba, Mumbai, Maharashtra 400039


Location:
Providing free WiFi, Carlton Hotel is located in Mumbai. Popular points of interest around the property include Gateway of India and Prince of Wales Museum. The property provides a 24-hour front desk. At the economy hotel, the rooms come with a closet. Each room is equipped with a flat-screen TV, and certain units at Carlton Hotel have a balcony. All rooms will provide guests with a fridge. Colaba Causeway is half a kilometer from the accommodation, while Rajabai Clock Tower is a 10-minute walk away. The nearest airport is Chhatrapati Shivaji International Airport, 19.3 km from the property.Colaba is a great choice for travelers interested inhistory,architectureandculture. This property also has one of the top-rated locations in Mumbai! Guests are happier about it compared to other properties in the area.
