QUTUB HEIGHTS DELHI

Asset No.6, Aerocity Hospitality District, IGI Airport


Location:
Flat no. 4, 2nd floor, 1090/1, Kalka Dass Marg, Above Axis Bank Ward no. 1, Mehrauli, New Delhi, 110030

Located in Mehrauli, New Delhi, Qutub heights provides accommodation in a service apartment witha fully equipped private bathroom with bath or shower and free toiletries,a seating area, a flat-screen TV and a kitchen. Complimentary WiFi is offered.

This service apartment in New Delhi isless than 1 km of Qutub Minar and 10 km of Lodhi Gardens in New Delhi,Lotus Temple is 10 km from the apartment, while Gandhi Smriti is 11 km from the property. The nearest airport is Delhi International Airport, 13 km from Qutub heights.

This property is ideal for family, group and couple stay.
