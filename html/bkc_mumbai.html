HOTEL BKC GARDEN MUMBAI

6th floor, Building No 11. S.R.A., Motilal Nehru Nagar, Near Trade Centre, Bkc Road, Bandra East, Mumbai 400051


Location:
Conveniently set in the Bandra district of Mumbai, Hotel Bkc Garden is located 5.6 km from Siddhi Vinayak Temple, 6.3 km from Pali Hill and 6.8 km from Phoenix Market City Mall. The property is around 8 km from Mumbai Domestic Terminal 1, 8 km from Prithvi Theatre and 9.7 km from Juhu Beach. The property provides a 24-hour front desk and room service for guests. At the hotel, every room is equipped with a desk, a flat-screen TV and a private bathroom. Guest rooms at Hotel Bkc Garden include air conditioning and a closet. High Street Phoenix mall is 11.3 km from the accommodation, while ISKCON is 11.3 km away. The nearest airport is Chhatrapati Sivaji International Airport, 8 km from Hotel Bkc Garden. Bandra is a great choice for travelers interested in nightlife, restaurants and food.
