HOTEL COSY GRAND DELHI

13, Aradhana Enclave, Sector 13, R.K.Puram, . 5 Minutes from the Embassy Area. South Delhi


Location:
Hotel Cosy Grand is situated in the epic city which echoes the chronicles of India, New Delhi. It is a luxury property, which offers a perfect blend of modernity with traditional values of warm hospitality, encircled by the excellent view of the city, well known for its prompt services and courteous staff. Quietly elegant and classic, the hotel features facilities like front desk, doctor on call, laundry, room service, Wi-Fi internet access, housekeeping and parking. Backed by a team of skilled professionals, the property ensures that all needs of guests are fulfilled timely.

The hotel offers accommodation with modern designs and provides perfect solutions to the needs of business and leisure traveler. Each room features a soothing colour palette with good quality of curtains and lights, comfortable upholstered furniture, elegant flooring that completes the oasis setting. Well fitted with contemporary amenities like television, telephone, air conditioning, wardrobe, comfy beds, soft linen, writing desk, tea/coffee maker, mini-bar and attached bathrooms with hot/cold water and essential toiletries. The rooms here are perfectly designed and crafted to make the stay relaxed and comfortable for the guests.

The multi-cuisine restaurant, is perfect for relaxed dining in breakfast, lunch and dinner. Guests can host seminars, meetings, conferences, social events like kitty/birthday parties, wedding and many more in well maintained conference/banquet room fitted with latest facilities and technology.

Located at a distance of 11 km from New Delhi, 6 km from AIIMS Metro Station, 2.6 km from R.K Puram Bus Terminal and 7.2 km from Indira Gandhi International Airport. Guests can explore various nearby tourist attractions like Hauz Khas Village(6 km), Red Fort(14.7 km), India Gate(8.5 km), Jantar Mantar(8.3 km), Humayun's Tomb(8.8 km), Lotus Temple(12.2 km) and many more.

Experience a grand stay at Hotel Cosy Grand!
