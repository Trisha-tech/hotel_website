MARINE BAY SANDS SINGAPORE

10 bayfront Avenue,Marina Bay singapore 018956


Location:
Discover Marina Bay Sands, a hotel of architectural wonder in the heart of thriving Singapore, crowned by the world's largest rooftop infinity pool.The hotel is linked directly to CE1/DT16 Bayfront Stationon the Circle Line and Downtown Line of Singapore's Mass Rapid Transit (MRT) train system. Changi Airport is approximately 20-minute direct drive away along the East Coast Parkway (ECP) expressway.

Imagine yourself at the floor-to-ceiling windows of your 5-star hotel room, drinking in magnificent views of Singapores glittering city district or picture-perfect Gardens by the Bay. Or swimming in the worlds highest and longest infinity pool with its unrivalled panoramic views a once-in-a-lifetime experience available exclusively to guests of Marina Bay Sands hotel. Indian and vegetarian food options can be found at Punjab Grill (Northern Indian restaurant located in the mall) and at Rasapura Foodmasters, located just across the hotel.

Marina Bay Sands boasts the world's largest rooftop pool stretching 150 metres across Sands SkyPark. Accessible only by hotel guests, the iconic Infinity Pool at Marina Bay Sands offers a breathtaking city-skyline view not found at any other Singapore hotel.

Be amazed by acclaimed museum exhibitions and breathtaking theatre shows, andexplore over 80 restaurants and bars and 170 premium brand stores, Singapore's largest casino, alllocated within the vicinity.At your fingertips, the best of spectacular nightlife and entertainment, celebrity chef dining and luxury shopping.

Rejuvenate yourself with the award-winning selection of massage and beauty treatments at Banyan Tree Spa. Gaze upon sparkling water views from 55 storeys above Marina Bay, as the blissful surrounds of Oriental-inspired water gardens and warm-toned private rooms soothe your mind and spirit. The hotel also hosts Banyan Tree Fitness Club where you can work out to inspiring views of Marina Bay from 55 storeys high.

Daily river ferry services connect to Bayfront South Jetty at Marina Bay Sands. The jetty is situated at the south Promenade, near to The Shoppes (3 minute walk), Sands Expo & Convention Centre (5 minute walk), and Hotel Tower 1 (8 minute walk).

Marina Bay Sands offers extensive and highly flexible meeting spaces. Sands Expoand Convention Centre is Singapores largest and most versatile meeting space, featuring over 120,000 square meters of flexible space, 250 meeting rooms, Southeast Asias largest ballroom and the capacity for 2,000 exhibition booths.
