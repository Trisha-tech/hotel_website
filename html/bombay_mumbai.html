RED FOX HOTEL DELHI AIRPORT

Asset No.6, Aerocity Hospitality District, IGI Airport


Location:
Located in the new cosmopolitan district - Delhi Aerocity, the hotel is a few minutes from the airport. Vasant Vihar, Diplomatic Enclave and Chanakyapuri are conveniently located close by from the hotel. The commercial hub of Gurugram and the entertainment center of Vasant Kunj are also within easy reach from Delhi Aerocity. This hotel welcomes you with its fresh bold interiors as well as crisp and clean rooms and delights you with its unbeatable value and reliable safety standards. Here friendly smiles and a lively environment go hand in hand with professional service. A hotel that is young at heart, Red Fox lets you be yourself whether you are working on your laptop or winning a game of carom in the lounge. Add to this, Clever Fox Cafs scrumptious food and the hotels' close to home environment - these hotels become the first choice for price conscious travelers. The hotel offers 207 smart rooms and suites, a multi-cuisine coffee shop Clever Fox Caf, a Cyber Kiosk, an efficient meeting room, a well-equipped fitness center and laundry service. Key highlights of the hotel are also its state-of-the-art security arrangements which include hi end solutions like crash bollards and boom barriers at each entrance; under vehicle surveillance system; baggage x-ray scanners; back-to-back CCTV coverage of all public areas; Fish Eye cameras in the basements that provide a 360 view; face recognition analytical cameras; a comprehensive video wall in the security control room to monitor all coverage simultaneously; a 3D walkthrough; bio-metric access control for all back-of-the-house areas; intruder alarm system and panic buttons at key locations like the main gate and reception. Needless to say the hotel is truly world class in all aspects.
