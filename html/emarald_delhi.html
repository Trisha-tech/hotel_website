EMARALD HOTEL DELHI

112, Babar Road,Near Bharakhamba Road,Opp. Hotel Lalit Gate No. 3, Connaught Place, New Delhi.110001


Location:
Location: Located on Babar Road in Connaught Place, Hotel Emarald is an elegant combination of service and efficiency. It is at a distance of 21 km from Indira Gandhi International Airport and 2 km from New Delhi Railway Station. Popular places to visit in the city are Red Fort (4 km), Humayun's Tomb (6 km), Lodhi Garden (7 km), Akshardham (7 km), Lotus Temple (13 km) and many more. Room Amenities: Hotel has luxurious and well furnished rooms with amenities like air conditioner, Wi-Fi access, television, tea and coffee maker and wardrobe. Each room has attached bathroom with regular supply of hot and cold water. Hotel Facilities: The hotel proffers modern facilities like room service, laundry service, front desk for assistance, conference services for corporate events and banquet facility for wedding ceremony and birthday parties and other social gathering. Dining: Guests can indulge themselves to scrumptious and delectable delicacies at the in-house multi cuisine restaurant wherein they can savor their taste buds. Adding to this is a pleasing ambiance and never-ending conversations which will sum up to a memorable experience.
