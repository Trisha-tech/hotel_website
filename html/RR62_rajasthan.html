HOTEL RR 62 RAJASTHAN

350, Chandi Ki Taksal, Near Kale Hanuman Ji Ka Mandir, Jaipur


Location:
Featuring a terrace, Hotel RR 62 is set in Jaipur, 1.2 km from Hawa Mahal - Palace of Winds. Featuring a shared lounge, the 4-star hotel has air-conditioned rooms with a private bathroom. The accommodation provides a 24-hour front desk, a shuttle service, a concierge service and free WiFi. All guest rooms at the hotel come with a seating area and a flat-screen TV. A continental or buffet breakfast is available each morning at the property. Guests can dine in the on-site restaurant, which serves a variety of International dishes. City Palace is 1.8 km from Hotel RR 62. Jaipur International Airport is 15 km from the property. This property is also rated for the best value in Jaipur! Guests are getting more for their money when compared to other properties in this city.
