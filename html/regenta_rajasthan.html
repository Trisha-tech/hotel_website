REGENTA CENTRAL JAIPUR

91,92 Ramnathpuri Jhotwara, Jaipur, Near Sikar road , Rajasthan, India
Regenta Central, Jaipur


Location:
Located close to many historical monuments and architectural marvels, Regenta Central, Jaipur is the hotel of choice for leisure travellers. The hotel comprises of 44 rooms divided into 28 Executive Club Rooms, 8 Club Rooms and 8 Executive Deluxe Rooms. Each room has amenities such as high-speed internet, tea/coffee maker and laundry services to make the travellers accommodation in Jaipur comfortable.

Pinxx, the in house restaurant cum resto-bar dishes out global delicacies that are offered in a fine dining setting blending contemporary style and rustic charm. Offering a picturesque view of Jaipur city, the Skylight, a rooftop bar at the hotel serves the best cocktail and mocktail in the city.

The hotel also houses two moderately sized banquet halls and one large lawn best suited for corporate conclaves, product launches, seminars, birthdays, family get-together, large weddings and receptions.

Hawa Mahal, Jal Mahal, and Albert Hall Museum are some of the few tourist spots close to the hotel.
