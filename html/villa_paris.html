VILLA PANTHEON

41, rue des Ecoles 75005 PARIS


Location:
Located in the 5th arrondissement of Paris, Villa Pantheon Hotel is easily reachable within a 10-minute walk from major attractions like the Pantheon, Luxembourg Palace and Odeon Theatre. Maubert - Mutualite metro station is just 2 minutes walk from the property while Charles de Gaulle Airport is roughly a 40-minute drive away. Modern meeting facilities are available within the premises for the comfort of corporate travellers. REVIEWS AND RATINGS


Friendly, helpful, brilliant location
markhC3794ZY, COUPLES Mar 18, 2020

My girlfriend and I arrived here just as the bars and restaurants were about to close down during the Coronavirus epidemic, but the staff were brilliant and helped us through it. We managed just one meal on the night we arrived at the excellent bistro next door before the lock down, and then had to rely on takeout food and drink from various cafes nearby. The team here helped with phone calls and locations, and a bottle opener when we had to resort to drinking takeout wine in the lobby!! Situated close to Notre Dame, the Sarbones and the Pantheon, there was plenty to explore on foot. The room was clean and the hotel had plenty of character. Whilst we had to cut our stay short, we’d like to go back when things return to normal.
