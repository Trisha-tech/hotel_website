SWISSOTEL MERCHANT COURT HOTEL SINGAPORE

20 Merchant Road, Singapore 058281


Location:
wissotel Merchant Court offers contemporary rooms, a 5-minute walk from nightlife options and Clark Quay MRT Station. Little India MRT station is just 2 stops away from the hotel. Overlooking the Singapore River, the hotelhas a landscaped pool, a 24-hour gym and award winning dining outlets.

Swissotel Merchant Court Singapore is a 25-minute drive from Changi International Airport. Orchard Road and the Central Business District are a 10-minute drive away.Swissotel Merchant Court Singapore features a restaurant and free WiFi. The hotel has an outdoor pool and views of the river, and guests can enjoy a drink at the bar.

Featuring modern furnishings, rooms at Merchant Court Swissotel have over-sized windows with clear views of the pool, river or city skyline. Enjoy the comfort of free tea/coffee making amenities. The en suite bathroom includes shower facilities, a hairdryer and free toiletries. Bedroom slippers are provided for your comfort. Guests can request for complimentary toothbrush/toothpaste set. All rooms are equipped with complimentary use of a handy smartphone equipped with unlimited mobile internet and complimentary IDD calls to selected countries.

All rooms come with a flat-screen TV with cable channels. Certain accommodations have a sitting area for your convenience. You will find a kettle in the room. Each room includes a private bathroom. For your comfort, you will find bathrobes, slippers and free toiletries.

You will find a 24-hour front desk at the property.

The hotel also provides car rental. Desaru is 43.5 km from Swissotel Merchant Court, and Nagoya is 25.7 km away. The nearest airport is Changi Airport, 17.7 km from Swissotel Merchant Court Singapore.

Hotel Highlights

Leisure - Singapore's first Swiss Spa, the 'Purove Spa & Sport' offers a swimming pool with water slides, outdoor Jacuzzi and a 24-hourfitness centre spread across 2 floors. Business - fully equipped business centre and multiple venues for meetings and events.
