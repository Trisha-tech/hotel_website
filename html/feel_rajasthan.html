HOTEL KAILASH ( Hotel Since 1932 )

AGRASEN MARKET, JOHARI BAZAAR, JAIPUR


Location:
Kailash Hotel is nestled in the pink city, Jaipur. The hotel is located at the distance of 5km from Jaipur Railway Station, 3 km from Sindhi Camp Bus Station and 12 km from Jaipur International Airport. Guests can visit the places like Jaipur Zoo(2 km), lbert Hall Museum(2 km), Rajmandir Cinema(3 km), Jantar Mantar(850 m) and many more.
Room Amenities:
The hotel features an array of amenities like comfy beds, clean linen, table, chairs, air condition, Wi-fi on demand and an attached bathroom with hot/cold water and an essential toiletries.
Hotel Facilities:
The hotel features facilities like front desk, pick and drop on demand, housekeeping, travel desk, sightseeing, doctor on call and many more.
Dining:
Guests can relish authentic food at the inhouse dining area of the hotel
