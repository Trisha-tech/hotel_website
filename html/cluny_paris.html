REVIEWS AND RATINGS


Excellent Place to Stay in Paris!
vcap98, FRIENDS Oct 23, 2019

I would highly recommend this hotel for anyone staying in Paris! It’s in a central location (walkable to sites like Notre Dame and the Louvre, very close to the metro as well) the prices are great, and the hotel staff is incredibly kind. Very clean and overall lovely atmosphere.
only if you budget is really limited
Curiosity498251, BUISNESS Sep 02, 2019

This is a 2-star hotel next to the Sorbonne, close to the Pantheon. On the plus-side, it is centrally located in Paris, in walking distance to the Quartier Latin. The service was friendly, and the room was clean. The room was really small, but this is to be expected for a 2-start hotel in Paris. On the negative side is the fact that you can hear any conversation/TV in the neighboring rooms, phonic isolation is terrible. The beds are of bad quality, I had severe backaches after staying there for 2 nights, and the rooms have no AC. So in summer you have the choice to open the window on a busy noisy street or suffocate in the heat. This hotel was chosen for me by business partners, but if given a choice, I would not go back.

Heptameron F, Jul 11, 2019

Perfect location for Latin Quarter/St-Germain/Notre-Dame
Linda K, COUPLES May 08, 2019

Handy little hotel, basic but very reasonable priced. The room was very small but clean. This is very much a base for people in the city to see the sights - you won't spend much time lounging in your room. The staff were very courteous and helpful, and the location means you're right off the Boul' Mich and five mins from the lively spots around Notre-Dame. If you're booking a romantic stay see if you can get one of the rooms at the front of the hotel - room 63 for example has great views and is bigger. We guessed that every floor's front room is similarly numbered - 23/33/43/53 - will all look out on the Sorbonne (but also might be a bit noisier). Many thanks to all the team at the Hotel - we had a great time in the city.
